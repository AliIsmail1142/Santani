Fine Dining Redefined

Food is an experience and we take it to heart by procuring the finest ingredients, prepared by chefs of immaculate calibre, bringing forth cuisine from across the world. We offer absolute authenticity, that will reinvigorate your tastebuds anew.

Santini Hotel has 3 restaurants, each with their own unique spin on fine dining.

View Menu

The Santani Kitchen

Our in-house restaurant, the Santani Kitchen, offers a delightful array of flavours. Our main attraction is our Continental Breakfast, that merges Mediterranean cuisine with European flavours. We also prepare a delectable collection of baked goods from Croissants to Danishes.

Opening Hours

Breakfast: 4.00am - 11.30am

View Menu

Rascals Bar & Grill

Rascals Bar & Grill provides a captivating dining experience with an emphasis on fresh ingredients, curated from farm to table. Specializing in comfort food with a refined twist, Rascals offers a vast menu that will wow any palate. From scrumptious burgers to crispy bites, Rascals has it all!

Opening Hours

Lunch: 12.00pm - 4.00pm

Dinner: 6.30pm - 12.30am

View Menu

Carmelo's Restaurant

Carmelo's Restaurant has a single goal. That is to bring to South-East Asia the most amazing gustatory sensations, all the way from Sicily in Italy. Carmelo's is Sicilian Cuisine at its finest, with award winning recipes curated by some of the best Sicilian chefs from Italy. If you are looking for an Italian Feast, look no further than Carmelo's.

Opening Hours

Lunch: 12.00pm - 3.30pm

Dinner: 5.30pm - 11.00pm
